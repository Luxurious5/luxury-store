What is Furniture of America?

Founded in 1990, Furniture of America is a leading manufacturer and distributor of stylish and affordable furniture. The brand puts a strong emphasis on modern design, quality craftsmanship, and exceptional customer service. Over the years, Furniture of America has built a reputation for providing a wide variety of furniture options for different styles and needs.

The History Behind Furniture of America

Furniture of America started its journey in the competitive world of furniture manufacturing over three decades ago. The company’s founders aimed to create stylish furniture for the masses. Today, they have succeeded in becoming a trusted household name. Their focus on innovation and design helps set them apart in the crowded market.

Understanding the Furniture of America Catalog

The Furniture of America catalog is an extensive collection that showcases a wide range of products, from sofas and beds to dining sets and accent tables. This catalog is designed not only to inform customers about the products available but also to inspire them by showcasing the latest trends and styles in furniture design.

What Can You Find in the Furniture of America Catalog?

The catalog is divided into various sections, each highlighting different categories. Here are some of the main categories you will find:

  • Living Room Furniture: Sofas, sectionals, coffee tables, and accent chairs.
  • Bedroom Furniture: Beds, nightstands, dressers, and armoires.
  • Dining Room Furniture: Dining tables, chairs, barstools, and buffets.
  • Office Furniture: Desks, office chairs, and storage solutions.
  • Accent Furniture: Ottomans, side tables, and shelves.

Styles Available in the Catalog

Furniture of America offers a wide range of styles to suit various tastes. Whether you prefer traditional, contemporary, or a mix of both, there's something for everyone in the catalog:

  • Modern: Clean lines and sleek designs that enhance the look of your space.
  • Rustic: A more natural and earthy feel, often using reclaimed wood and warm colors.
  • Industrial: Inspired by urban lofts, featuring metal accents and vintage finishes.
  • Farmhouse: Cozy and inviting designs with a touch of nostalgia.
  • Traditional: Classic designs that never go out of style, often featuring rich fabrics and intricate details.

Why Choose Furniture of America?

When it comes to furniture, you have many options available, so why should you choose Furniture of America? Here’s a look at some of the key benefits:

1. Quality Craftsmanship

Furniture of America prides itself on creating high-quality pieces. They use durable materials and expert craftsmanship to ensure that their furniture lasts for years.

2. Wide Range of Options

The catalog features a vast selection of products, making it easy to find something that fits your style and budget.

3. Affordable Pricing

One of the brand’s primary missions is to offer stylish furniture at reasonable prices. You don’t have to break the bank to furnish your home!

4. Trendy Designs

Furniture of America keeps up with the latest trends in home decor. Their catalog is filled with fashionable pieces that can elevate your space.

5. Customer Support

The company provides excellent customer service, ensuring that your purchasing experience is as smooth as possible. Whether you have a question about a product or need assistance with shipping, their team is ready to help.

Tips for Navigating the Furniture of America Catalog

With so many options available in the catalog, it can be overwhelming to make a decision. Here are some tips to help you navigate the catalog effectively:

1. Define Your Style

Before you start browsing, take a moment to define your style. Look for inspiration on platforms like Pinterest or Instagram to see what resonates with you. Knowing your preference will make it easier to find pieces you love.

2. Measure Your Space

Before making a purchase, measure the area where you plan to place the furniture. This will help you avoid buying items that are too large or small for your space. Having these measurements handy will also assist you in creating a balanced layout.

3. Prioritize Comfort

While style is important, comfort should not be overlooked. For example, when choosing a sofa, sit on it to test its comfort level. You want to invest in pieces that you and your family will enjoy for years to come.

4. Consider the Color Scheme

Pay attention to the color palette of your room. Ensure that the furniture you choose complements or enhances your existing decor. Neutral colors provide versatility, while bold colors can serve as statement pieces.

5. Plan for Functionality

Think about how you will use the space. If you entertain frequently, consider investing in larger dining sets or ample seating in your living room. Choose pieces that serve multiple purposes, like coffee tables with storage options.

How to Purchase from the Furniture of America Catalog

Once you’ve selected the perfect pieces from the catalog, it’s time to make a purchase. Here are the steps to follow:

1. Explore the Catalog

Take your time exploring the Furniture of America catalog. You can view it online or request a physical copy, depending on your preference.

2. Check Retailers

Furniture of America products are available at various retailers. Check their website for a list of authorized sellers near you.

3. Read Product Reviews

Before making a final decision, read customer reviews. This will give you insight into the quality and performance of the furniture. Reviews often highlight both the positives and any potential drawbacks.

4. Make Your Purchase

Once you’ve found your desired items, follow the retailer’s purchasing guide to finalize your order. Don’t forget to inquire about shipping options and delivery times!

5. Enjoy Your New Furniture!

Once your items arrive, take the time to set them up and enjoy your new furniture. A well-furnished home not only looks great but also enhances your quality of life.

Conclusion

The Furniture of America catalog is a fantastic resource for anyone looking to furnish their home with quality and stylish pieces. With a diverse selection of products, affordable prices, and a focus on customer satisfaction, it’s no wonder that Furniture of America has become a household name. By following the tips in this guide, you’ll be well on your way to decorating your space exactly as you imagined. Happy shopping!
